# Polls, Chat & Resources

Working with the chat log and polls that came from the original meeting.

> Developer-facing twin: [../specs/polls-chat-resources.spec.md](../specs/polls-chat-resources.spec.md)

## What this gives you

**Imported chat and polls.** If the original meeting produced a chat log or
polls, they are carried into the session and appear in the Editor's right rail
under the Chat and Polls tabs.

**Place them in the timeline.** Drag a chat or poll card onto the transcript
segment where it belongs. The card snaps to that segment's start time and shows a
"PLACED · Slide N" badge. Placed cards render inline in the transcript as
bordered blocks.

**Re-anchor or detach.** Drag a placed card again to move it, or drag it back to
the right rail to detach it. The unplaced card returns to the list.

**Automatic first-pass placement.** Polls get an initial automatic placement
based on where they fit best in the transcript, so you start with a reasonable
layout and only adjust the ones that look off.

**Reorder.** Both chat messages and polls can be reordered within their lists.

**Participant list.** The system collects everyone who appears in the chat so you
can see the participants at a glance.

You do not have to place everything — most chat is context only. Place the
notable questions and key moments; leave the rest unplaced.

## Known gaps

- **Chat and polls are read-only** — you can move or detach them, but not edit
  their text or options.
- **No bulk placement** — cards are placed one at a time.
- **No "show only placed / unplaced" filter.**
- **No reactions or emoji.**
- **Chat timestamps are not shown** in the card — author and text only.
